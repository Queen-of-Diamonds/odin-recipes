Oven Beef Stew

Description

Stay warm during the cold winter months with this delicious Oven Beef Stew. This stew is healthy, nutritious and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1kg stewing beef
  • 50g flour
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1 teaspoon Hinds seasoning
  • 3 tablespoons sunflower oil
  • 2 cups of boiling hot water
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • a handful of frozen green beans
  • a handful of frozen peas
Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees
  2. Put the flour on a plate
  3. Add white pepper and Hinds seasoning to the flour
  4. Add oil to the frying pan
  5. Coat the meat in the seasoned flour
  6. Fry the meat until brown on both sides
  7. Place the fried meat, carrots and 2 cups of boiling hot water into an oven dish
  8. Place in the oven and let it cook for 1 hour
  9. After 1 hour, add potatoes, green beans, peas and beef stock cube
  10. Place the stew back into the oven and let it cook for 1 hour
  11. Thicken the stew with cornflour if needed

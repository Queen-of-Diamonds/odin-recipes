Chicken Stir-Fry

Description

This bright and colourful stir fry is easy to prepare and great for a weeknight feast

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp chicken seasoning
  • 350g boneless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into strips
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • noodles seasoning packets
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons sunflower oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger and garlic paste
  • 700g McCain Frozen Hawaiian Stir Fry
  • noodles
Steps
  1. Place chicken in a bowl and add black pepper, garlic powder, cornstarch and chicken seasoning
  2. Let it marinade for 30 minutes
  3. To make the sweet and sour sauce, add 1 1/2 cups of water in a jug
  4. Add in the vinegar, sugar, contents of the noodle seasoning packets, tomato sauce and cornstarch
  5. Stir the sauce well and set aside
  6. In a pan, heat up the oil
  7. Fry the marinated chicken strips until brown and set aside
  8. In the same pan, fry the onions and ginger and garlic paste
  9. Add in the frozen stir-fry vegetables and let it cook through
  10. Add in the chicken strips and sweet and sour sauce and let it simmer until the sauce thickens
  11. Prepare noodles according to packaging instructions
  12. Serve with the stir-fry sauce
